Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 15, 2026

TalkTime is a voice-only social calling app for adults (18+). These Community Guidelines apply to all users—callers and hosts— on Android and iOS. By using TalkTime, you agree to follow these rules.

Zero tolerance: Nudity, sexual content, sexual conversation, pornography, and any content involving minors are strictly prohibited. Violations can result in immediate permanent ban without refund of unused coins.

1. Age requirement

  • You must be 18 years of age or older to create an account or use TalkTime.
  • Misrepresenting your age, or helping a minor use the app, is forbidden.
  • Accounts we reasonably believe belong to minors will be removed.

2. No sexual content, nudity, or adult solicitation

TalkTime is not a dating, adult, or cam platform. You may not:

  • Engage in sexual talk, erotic roleplay, sexting, or adult sexual conversation on voice calls.
  • Request, offer, or discuss sexual services, “pay for intimacy,” or pornographic content.
  • Upload, share, or request nude, partially nude, sexually suggestive, or pornographic images (including profile photos, KYC/selfie misuse, or any other upload).
  • Use usernames, bios, display names, or profile text that are sexual, explicit, or pornographic.
  • Harass others with unwanted sexual advances or pressure them into sexual topics.

Friendly, respectful, non-sexual conversation (languages practice, hobbies, everyday chat) is welcome. Sexual or NSFW conversation is not.

3. No child sexual abuse or exploitation

Any sexual content involving minors, CSAM, grooming, or exploitation is banned and may be reported to authorities. See our Child Safety Standards.

4. Respect and safety

  • No abuse, threats, hate speech, bullying, stalking, or harassment.
  • No discrimination based on gender, caste, religion, race, nationality, disability, or similar protected characteristics.
  • No doxxing or sharing someone else’s private information without consent.
  • No impersonation of another person, brand, or official TalkTime staff.
  • No scams, phishing, or attempts to steal accounts, money, or personal data.

5. Voice-only product rules

  • TalkTime calls are voice-only. Do not attempt to use the app for video intimacy, nude streaming, or sexual performance.
  • Do not record calls without the other person’s clear consent where required by law.
  • Hosts must keep profiles and verification materials accurate and appropriate.

6. Spam, ads, and commercial misuse

  • No spam calls, mass promotion, or unsolicited advertising.
  • No selling or promoting illegal goods/services, drugs, weapons, or adult services.
  • No multi-level marketing / crypto scams / investment schemes pitched on calls.
  • Do not ask users to move payments off the App Store / Google Play to evade platform rules.

7. Honest accounts and fair use of coins

  • One person = one account (unless we expressly allow otherwise).
  • Do not abuse promo systems, chargebacks, purchase exploits, or wallet fraud.
  • Do not pressure hosts or callers for off-app cash payments tied to TalkTime services.

8. Reporting and enforcement

If someone breaks these rules:

  • Use in-app Report after a call or on a profile where available.
  • Or email talktime717@gmail.com with the username, what happened, and approximate date/time.
  • For child-safety emergencies, contact local law enforcement first, then report to us (see Child Safety Standards).

We may take action including warnings, temporary suspension, permanent ban, coin forfeiture, removal of host status, and reporting to Apple, Google, or law enforcement when appropriate.

9. Advertising and tracking

TalkTime does not currently show third-party ads in the app. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. See our Privacy Policy for how data is used.

10. Updates

We may update these guidelines. Continued use of TalkTime after updates means you accept the current version. Related documents: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Child Safety Standards.

11. Contact

Questions or reports: talktime717@gmail.com · Support page
