TalkTime

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

TalkTime (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the TalkTime Android application (com.matez.voice) that lets callers browse hosts and place voice-only calls using in-app coins. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use TalkTime, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address and authentication identifiers when you sign up or sign in (including guest/anonymous sessions where enabled).
  • Profile information: display name, username, phone number, bio, profile photo, languages, gender, date of birth, and location fields you choose to provide (e.g. state, district).
  • Host verification (hosts only): identity documents and selfie images submitted for host application review (e.g. Aadhaar front/back, selfie).
  • Call and usage data: call history, call status, duration, timestamps, and online/presence status while you use the app.
  • Virtual currency: coin wallet balance, coin transaction history, and in-app purchase records.
  • Payment data: Google Play purchase tokens and order metadata for coin recharges. Payment card details are processed by Google Play; we do not receive your full payment card number.
  • Voice sessions: audio is transmitted in real time during calls via our voice provider (Agora). We do not intentionally record or store call audio unless required for safety, legal compliance, or with your consent.
  • Device and technical data: basic device/app information needed to operate the service, secure accounts, and prevent abuse.

2. How we use information

We use your information to:

  • Provide voice calling, host discovery, and coin-based billing.
  • Create and manage your account and profile.
  • Process Google Play purchases and credit coins to your wallet.
  • Review host applications and enforce community and safety rules.
  • Show availability status (e.g. online, on a call) to other users as designed in the app.
  • Respond to support requests and improve the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect users from fraud or abuse.

3. How we share information

We share information only as needed to run TalkTime:

  • Service providers: Supabase (authentication, database, storage), Agora (real-time voice), and Google Play (in-app purchases).
  • Other users: profile details you make visible (such as display name, photo, languages, and online status) may be shown to other users in the app.
  • Legal and safety: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active and as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, enforce our terms, and meet legal requirements. When you delete your account, we delete associated personal data as described on our Account Deletion page.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access and export: use Settings → Export My Data in the app to receive a copy of your data.
  • Delete account: use Settings → Delete Account or follow the steps on our Account Deletion page.
  • Profile controls: update or remove optional profile fields in the app where available.
  • Permissions: you can manage microphone permission in your device settings. Voice calls require microphone access.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (such as access, correction, or objection). Contact us using the email below to exercise those rights.

6. Children

TalkTime is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and access controls on our backend. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards when data is transferred internationally.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of TalkTime after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data: talktime717@gmail.com
