Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 17, 2025

You can permanently delete your TalkTime account and associated personal data at any time. Deletion is irreversible. After deletion, you will lose access to your profile, coin balance, call history, and any host application materials linked to your account.

Before you delete: Unused coins are forfeited and cannot be refunded after account deletion. If you need a copy of your data first, use Settings → Export My Data in the app.

Delete account in the app (recommended)

  1. Open the TalkTime app on your Android or iPhone.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to Profile → Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm when prompted. Your account and data will be permanently removed.

You must be signed in to delete your account from within the app. The deletion completes immediately after confirmation.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address linked to your TalkTime account:

talktime717@gmail.com

Include:

  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • The email address registered to your TalkTime account
  • A brief statement that you want your account permanently deleted

We may ask you to verify ownership before processing the request. We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your authentication account and profile (name, username, bio, photo, and other profile fields)
  • Coin wallet balance and coin transaction history
  • Call history associated with your account
  • In-app purchase records linked to your user ID
  • Host application documents and images (if you applied to become a host)

What may be retained

We may retain limited information only where required by law, to prevent fraud, resolve disputes, or enforce our terms—for example, anonymized or aggregated analytics, or records we are legally required to keep. Google Play and the App Store maintain their own records of transactions processed through their stores; those are subject to Apple’s or Google’s policies.

Real-time voice during calls is not stored by TalkTime as recordings. Deletion removes your account data from our systems as described above.

Questions

For privacy questions or help with deletion, contact talktime717@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy or Support page.
